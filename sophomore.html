Nights at Mikaela’s02

With the chaos that was my sophomore year, I often needed a breather from constant group studying, organization work, and even from going out with friends.

In order to get that breather I'd go to my friend Mikaela's apartment. Almost anytime I'd stop by just to have some company to do whatever. Maybe to eat, or maybe to watch videos, to chill, to study, to take naps, really anything. It was a place me and a group of friends could call their second home in Berkeley.
