Coming back to LA from Japan, first thing I did was go to 88Rising’s Head in the Clouds Festival. Rich Brian and Niki were two artists I became fans of during college, and seeing them perform two of my favorite songs (I Like U and Glow Like Dat) was insane.

2020 New Year's Resolution03

2020 New Year’s resolutions were pretty standard—one of which being just to make more of an effort to hang out with friends despite the chaos that is going through undergrad. Back-to-back. Time for old friends, time for new friends. Of course, it was cut short with the start of the pandemic, but I'm glad that they're the final memories I have from Berkeley.

Late Nights at the Parker House02

2am, working on a CS project, one of my roommates turns on the switch and starts playing some Smash Ultimate. 10 minutes later, my other roommate joins in and they start going against each other.

They hit me with the question, "are you trying to play some Smash?"

I look at the work I still have to do, and think to myself "I can play a few games."

We go to bed at 6am.

Open Gym Volleyball01

I remember checking the hours on the RSF website, prepping my gym clothes, going to the courts, and asking if I could hop in. And just like that, I remebered why I used to enjoy playing volleyball.

Playing volleyball was a time where I didn't worry about studying, try to find time to hang out with friends, do something for a club I'm a part of— it was time for myself. Eventually remembering peoples faces, becoming friends, learning from each other, and slowly becoming better at the game was something so satisfactory.
