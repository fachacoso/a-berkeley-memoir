The LaidBackStream05

Streaming started off as a meme and was supposed to document my "Road to Gold" in League, but eventually Genshin came out and I just kept streaming. Initially starting off with a few friends watching, I eventually acttracted random people who became regulars, fellow streamers, some old friends who wanted to see what I was up to, and even some of my students who always wondered why I had a backdrop while teaching. I eventually stopped to focus on other things, but it was fun to still have some late night memories despite the pandemic.

uGSI for Data 804

I love teaching. Giving students an even better experience than what I had when I was a student, guiding them through topics I've spent semesters practicing, sharing the things I struggled with, and giving them the confidence to succeed in Data 8 no matter who they were. While sometimes the needs of students aren't met in education, taking that responsibility, especially in I course I love, was fulfilling.

Yes, Chef!03

Alongside the time wandering in the grocery, cooking has been therapeutic, calming, and most importantly fun. Getting on video calls with friends as I embark on the journey of creating the final dish, feeling a little adventerous and ending up failing, and eating food that I create myself is lit.

Shout out to cooking youtubers.
